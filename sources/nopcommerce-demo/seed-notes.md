# Seed Notes

This public demo does not require demo credentials for the first planned task family.

## Stable Public Entities

Known products and pages visible on the storefront include:

- `Build your own computer`
- `Apple MacBook Pro`
- `HTC smartphone`
- `$25 Virtual Gift Card`

Stable page types include:

- storefront home
- search
- shopping cart
- configurable product pages

## Task Assumptions

- Product names remain stable enough for search and navigation tasks.
- Cart-related tasks should be bounded and verified at record time because other users may have changed shared demo state.
