# Task Ideas

Initial candidate queue:

- Open a named category such as `Electronics` or `Apparel`.
- Search for a known product such as `MacBook`.
- Open the `Build your own computer` product page.
- Add an item to cart and stop before checkout.
- Add an item to compare or wishlist and verify the resulting state.

Preferred first nopCommerce tasks:

1. home -> category
2. search -> product results
3. home or product -> cart -> stop before checkout

These are the strongest first tasks because they are modern retail workflows, independently verifiable, and aligned with the dataset matrix's modern-app e-commerce slice.
