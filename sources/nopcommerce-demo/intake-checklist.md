# Intake Checklist

- Confirm the site's legal and redistribution classification.
- Assign the correct `source_track`, `hosting_mode`, `reset_strategy`, and `determinism_level`.
- Confirm whether the site is stable enough for repeatable capture.
- Record whether demo credentials are required.
- Fill `bootstrap.md`, `reset-notes.md`, and `seed-notes.md` if the source is stateful or self-hosted.
- Add at least one entry point to `site.yaml`.
- Add at least three candidate task ideas before recording tasks.
