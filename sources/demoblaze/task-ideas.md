# Task Ideas

Initial candidate queue:

- Open a named category such as `Laptops`.
- Open a specific product detail page.
- Add an item to the cart and stop before purchase.
- Remove an item from the cart.
- Open the contact modal and stop there.

Preferred first Demoblaze tasks:

1. home -> category
2. home -> product detail
3. product or home -> cart -> stop before purchase

These are the strongest first tasks because they are easy to verify, cover retail behavior that the benchmark needs, and remain bounded.
