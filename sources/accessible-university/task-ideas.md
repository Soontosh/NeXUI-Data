# Task Ideas

Initial candidate queue:

- Open the `After` page from the overview page and stop there.
- Open the `Before` page from the overview page and stop there.
- Open the `Info` page and stop there.
- Navigate to a named content area on the AU home page and explain its surrounding structure.
- Compare a `Before` and `After` destination pair in terms of accessible navigation and page structure.

Preferred first recorded AU tasks:

1. overview -> after
2. overview -> before
3. overview -> info

These are the cleanest first tasks because they are deterministic, accessibility-centered, and independently verifiable from the final page title and URL.
